# Synthetic Data Generation

HarnessAI can create synthetic data for testing, demonstrations, training examples, evaluation workflows, and product development.

Synthetic data is artificial data that is designed to resemble real data without directly exposing real people, customers, students, patients, employees, or confidential business records.

Good uses include demo datasets, test cases, edge-case examples, workflow simulations, training materials, and early software development when real data is unavailable, sensitive, messy, or too limited.

Synthetic data can also help teams test whether an AI workflow behaves reasonably before connecting it to real operational data.

Synthetic data is not automatically safe or valid. It must be reviewed for realism, bias, leakage risk, and fitness for the intended use. It should not be treated as a substitute for real validation when real-world accuracy matters.

HarnessAI can help define the schema, generate representative examples, document assumptions, and create datasets that support practical testing without unnecessary exposure of sensitive information.
