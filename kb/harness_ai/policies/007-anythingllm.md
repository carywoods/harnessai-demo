# White-Labeled AnythingLLM

HarnessAI can help organizations deploy private or branded AI knowledge assistants using AnythingLLM.

AnythingLLM is useful when an organization wants a question-answering system over its own documents, procedures, policies, notes, or reference material. A deployment can be configured with different model providers, including commercial APIs or open-source models, depending on the organization's needs.

A white-labeled deployment can give the organization a branded interface, controlled document collections, and a focused assistant for internal or public-facing use cases.

Common use cases include staff knowledge assistants, internal documentation search, board or committee reference tools, training support, customer service knowledge bases, and project-specific research workspaces.

HarnessAI's role can include setup, configuration, document preparation, model selection, deployment planning, user training, and ongoing support.

AnythingLLM is not a magic replacement for information management. The quality of the assistant depends on the quality, organization, and permissions of the underlying documents.
