# HarnessAI Services Overview

HarnessAI offers practical AI services for organizations that want useful systems, not vague transformation projects.

The main service areas are CommPilot, white-labeled AnythingLLM deployments, AI consulting, and synthetic data generation.

CommPilot is an email-based Digital Front Desk for routine organizational questions and routing.

White-labeled AnythingLLM deployments give organizations a private or branded AI knowledge assistant that can answer questions from approved documents.

AI consulting helps organizations decide where AI fits, what workflow should be improved first, what model or vendor to use, and what risks or limits should be addressed before deployment.

Synthetic data generation creates artificial but realistic data for demos, testing, workflow simulation, edge-case exploration, and development when real data is sensitive, sparse, unavailable, or inconvenient to use.

HarnessAI prefers small, bounded first deployments. The best first project is narrow enough to launch, observe, improve, and explain.
