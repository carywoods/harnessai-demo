# CommPilot Overview

CommPilot is an email-based Digital Front Desk for organizations. It reads incoming messages, classifies the request, searches an organization-specific knowledge base, and drafts or sends a natural-language response.

CommPilot is designed for routine administrative communication. It is useful when an organization receives repeated questions about policies, hours, procedures, contacts, routing, services, or next steps.

The core idea is simple: people already know how to send email. CommPilot uses that familiar channel as the interface for an AI-powered information desk.

CommPilot is not intended to replace staff. It is intended to reduce repetitive answering, improve consistency, support multiple languages, and help people get useful information faster.

Good fits include school districts, nonprofits, associations, small businesses, local service organizations, and teams with frequent public-facing questions.

Poor fits include workflows that require access to sensitive records, legally binding decisions, emergency response, medical advice, financial advice, disciplinary decisions, or complex case-by-case judgment without human review.
