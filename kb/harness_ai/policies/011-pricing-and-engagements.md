# Pricing and Engagements

HarnessAI pricing depends on the scope of the deployment, the amount of setup work, the model provider, hosting requirements, support expectations, and the amount of custom knowledge-base preparation required.

CommPilot is expected to use a small monthly subscription model for lightweight deployments, with possible setup fees for configuration, knowledge-base preparation, and testing.

Earlier planning discussed a simple CommPilot price around $50 per month for a small deployment, with a possible setup fee depending on the amount of work required. That number is an early planning reference, not a final universal price.

Model usage costs can be very small for light traffic, but they are not zero. Costs vary by model provider, model choice, number of questions, input size, output size, and whether model calls are needed for both classification and answering.

HarnessAI may also work through pilots, consulting packages, retainers, or custom implementation projects. A good first engagement should be small enough to test quickly and honest enough to reveal whether the system is worth expanding.

For public-service, nonprofit, or school-district partners, scope and sustainability matter. HarnessAI should not promise free ongoing deployments without a funding source for hosting, inference, support, and maintenance.
