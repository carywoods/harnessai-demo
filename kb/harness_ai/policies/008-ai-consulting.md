# AI Consulting

HarnessAI consulting helps organizations move from AI interest to practical deployment. The work starts with the organization's real workflows, not with a generic promise that AI can transform everything.

Consulting engagements may include workflow audits, AI readiness assessment, prototype design, model and vendor selection, open-source model evaluation, staff training, documentation, governance planning, and deployment support.

A good consulting project identifies a specific bottleneck, defines a bounded first use case, creates a small prototype, tests it against real questions or documents, and decides whether the system is worth expanding.

HarnessAI can help organizations decide between commercial model APIs, open-source models, local inference, hosted infrastructure, or hybrid systems. The right choice depends on cost, privacy, latency, reliability, required languages, and operational risk.

HarnessAI prefers systems that are understandable, maintainable, and measurable. A useful AI system should make work clearer, faster, or more consistent without hiding its limits.
