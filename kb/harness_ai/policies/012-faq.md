# Frequently Asked Questions

## What does HarnessAI do?

HarnessAI builds practical AI systems for organizations, including CommPilot, white-labeled knowledge assistants, AI consulting, and synthetic data generation.

## What is CommPilot?

CommPilot is an email-based Digital Front Desk that answers routine organizational questions using an approved knowledge base.

## Does CommPilot replace staff?

No. CommPilot is intended to reduce repetitive communication work and improve consistency. It should escalate uncertain, sensitive, or out-of-scope questions to a person.

## Can CommPilot answer in multiple languages?

Yes, when configured with suitable model-based language handling. The Pike demo has tested English, Spanish, French, and Haitian Creole.

## Do we need technical staff?

Not necessarily for a small pilot. HarnessAI can help prepare the knowledge base, configure the system, deploy it, and document the workflow.

## What information does HarnessAI need to start?

A small first project needs approved source documents, a list of common questions, the intended audience, escalation contacts, and a clear list of topics the system should not answer.

## Can this be self-hosted?

Depending on the project, deployments may be hosted, self-hosted, or hybrid. The right choice depends on cost, privacy, reliability, model choice, and support needs.

## What happens when the AI does not know?

The system should say it does not know based on the available knowledge base and provide a contact or escalation path when possible.

## Is this secure?

Security depends on the deployment design, model provider, data boundaries, mailbox setup, access control, and monitoring. HarnessAI favors narrow pilots with separate mailboxes and approved knowledge bases.

## How fast can we launch?

A small demo can be assembled quickly when the source material is ready. A production deployment requires more testing, review, logging, and operating rules.
